Sample career posts

Three post concepts I would create for Cornell Career Services.

These are sample directions that can become Instagram carousels, short reels, or story prompts for @cornellcareerservices.

Carousel: use Cornell Career Network before you need a job

Goal: encourage students to explore alumni profiles early, without feeling overwhelmed.

Reel: first networking message formula

Goal: make networking feel specific, respectful, and less awkward.

Carousel: translate international experience

Goal: help international students explain scope, action, and impact for U.S. audiences.

Sample caption style: Not sure where to start with Cornell Career Network? Start before it feels urgent. This week, try saving three alumni profiles in a field you are curious about. You do not need the perfect question yet. You only need one next step.
